THE STORY

the story

The Bar

The Budgie Bar started as a cocktail bar in Ringwood, Victoria. The bar features an open floor plan with a sun-filled beer garden, concrete bar and a Palm Springs inspired cacti corner, The Budgie is the perfect place for your Friday after-work drinks or for a Saturday catch-up with friends.

The Present

And now here you are, looking at The Budgie Bar website. You can order one of our delicious bottled cocktails, or create your personal bottle. Then The Budgie Bar bottle will arrive on your doorstep, pre-made and pre-mixed, specially ready for you to enjoy on a nice summer's day or cold wintery night.

The Bottled Cocktail

But The Budgie Bar wanted bigger things. We decided to bottle our famous cocktails and sell them in store and online for all of Australia to enjoy from home. We also want to inspire creativity in our customers by letting you become the mixologist of a custom cocktail; right here on our website.

THE RANGE

the range

Espresso Martini

A perfect mixture of vodka, creme de cafe and cold drip coffee from our friends at coffee for the people. This drink is sure to wake you up and have you begging for another.

Coffee & Cigars

A beverage to warm the soul. Starting strong with a 12 year old scotch whisky, you move through delicate coffee notes to finish with subtle hints of orange - use this classic-style, but new-age cocktail to kick off your night or settle with a night cap.

Rum Old Fashioned

Now we're talking. The Old Fashioned, the original cocktail: spirit, sugar, bitters and water... What a combo! At Budgie Bar, you know it can't be the standard - we love it with rum! Simple yet elegant, this drink will have you dreading an empty glass.

Negroni Classico

Named after The Count himself, this bitter concoction is a divisive flavour in the cocktail world but a negroni done right is a beautiful thing. Those after something bitter, delicate and delicious know that this is the drink for them.

Classic Martini

A classic for a reason, this perfectly blended martini is the classiest way to enjoy a drink. A simple cocktail with a complex taste makes a normal night feel special. Serve it as cold as possible and it will guarantee to make you smile.
